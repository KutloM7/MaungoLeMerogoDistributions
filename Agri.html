About-Us

Maungo le Merogo Distributions is a locally owned business under the Direction of Pamela Motswisi. We are based in Selibe Phikwe and operate farms in Tsetsebjwe,Mmadinare,Gulushabe and Kasane. We distribute Vegetables such as, Green Peppers,Tomatoes,Cabage,Beetroot,Potatoes,Carrots (etc).
